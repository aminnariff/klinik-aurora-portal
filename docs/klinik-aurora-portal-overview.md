# Klinik Aurora — Clinic Management Portal

> An all-in-one web portal to manage your clinic's operations — patients, appointments, payments, staff, services, and promotions — from a single dashboard.

---

## What It Does

Klinik Aurora is a complete back-office system for clinic groups and standalone clinics. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, phone calls, and paper records, you get one login that shows you everything happening across your business.

---

## Feature Overview

### 1. Dashboard — Your Business at a Glance

See the metrics that matter most the moment you log in:

- Total registered patients
- Number of active branches
- Active promotions running
- Daily patient registration trends (last 7 days)
- Branch performance comparisons (for group admins)
- Export branch data to CSV for offline reporting

**Why it matters:** No more asking "how many patients came in today?" — the answer is already on screen.

### 2. Patient Management

A complete directory of every patient who has registered:

- Search and browse patients with pagination
- View full patient profiles (name, contact, registration date)
- See each patient's appointment history
- Track loyalty points balance and transaction history
- View all appointment references linked to a patient

**Why it matters:** Everything about a patient in one place — no digging through files.

> `[Screenshot: Patient detail view showing profile, points, appointment history]`

### 3. Appointment Management

Full control over your clinic's appointment pipeline:

- View all appointments with status filters
- Statuses tracked: Booked, Cancelled, Rescheduled, Pending Payment, Completed, Refunded, No Show, Transferred
- Create new appointments manually
- View appointment details — patient, doctor, branch, time
- Date-range dashboard for appointment statistics
- Link payments directly to appointments
- WhatsApp integration for appointment reminders and notifications

**Why it matters:** Reduce no-shows, track every stage of the patient journey, and keep communication automated.
